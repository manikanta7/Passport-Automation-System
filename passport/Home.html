Latest Update

August 2016

Four ID proofs ( Aadhaar, PAN, EPIC, Affidavit in format of Annexure-I) can get you a passport in 7 days.

Steps to Apply

* If you are an existing user, login to your account for availing our services.

 

* If you are a new user, register to our site by clicking on the "Register" button on the top.

 

* After logging in, fill up the application form accurately and submit it.

 

* Click on the Payment link to pay the appropriate fee for passport.

 

* Click on the Fix Appointment link to fix an appointment date.

 

* Print the application receipt which contains your Application Reference Number.

 

* Go to the passport office on the appointed day with the required documents.

 

* Once the verification process is successful, you can recieve the passport by courier in the next 10 days.
