Welcome User,

The various services offered are as follows:

 

* Fill Application Form:
Here you can fill up the online application form which is to be submitted for initiating the passport issuing process.

 

* Check Status:
If you have already submitted the application form, you can enter the application number and check the status of execution of your passport application.

 

* Appointment Date Availability(Apt Date):
If you have already submitted the online application form and if it has been processed by the passport administrator and the Regional Administrator , then you can check for the appointment date for police verification of your original documents at the nearest police station.

 

* Payment:
Here you can pay the application fee required for processing your application. Payment should be made once your application status changes from 'R' to 'p'. It indicates that the verification process is complete. Only CC/DC cards are accepted.
